What You Can Expect

So you're still reading. Good. Before you dive in, here's what this program actually looks like in practice -- because you deserve to know what you might be signing up for.

Training

The commitment is smaller than you think. About one hour per week of intense exercise and roughly 90 minutes per week of low-intensity exercise like walking on an incline or easy cycling. No two-a-days, no six-day splits, no living at the gym.

Diet

No need to count calories, eliminate food groups, or say goodbye to your favorite foods. You won't be eating boiled chicken and broccoli out of tupperware. You'll learn a sustainable approach that works with your life, not against it.

Results

They will come slowly. That's not a warning -- it's a feature. You won't see dramatic changes in a week, but you will see consistent, measurable progress month after month. Small gains will compound. Six months from now, the numbers will speak for themselves.
