VO2 Max

Cardiovascular fitness and its direct impact on mortality risk

The Science

VO2 max (maximal oxygen uptake) is the gold standard measure of cardiovascular fitness. It represents the maximum amount of oxygen your body can utilize during intense exercise and is one of the strongest predictors of all-cause mortality.

Mortality risk decreases substantially with each increase in VO2 max quartile
